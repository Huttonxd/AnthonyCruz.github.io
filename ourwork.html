My Work

At our last house cleaning. We begin by removing unnecessary objects and organizing the remaining elements in their corresponding place. We then wipe surfaces with a damp cloth or feather duster to remove dust and vacuum all rooms, paying particular attention to high-traffic areas and carpets. We use specific products to clean surfaces, such as the bathroom and kitchen, paying attention to the dirtiest areas. Finally, we use disinfectant products to eliminate germs and bacteria in the areas of greatest contact, such as doorknobs and faucets, and we check that all areas have been properly cleaned and disinfected.
